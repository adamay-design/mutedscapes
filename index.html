What's Mutedscapes?

This is a project that communicates the charm of the scenery reflected in buildings, which is often overlooked, through time-lapse and photos.

Generally, buildings are often perceived as inorganic and cold, giving an impression of being unaffected by the passage of time. However, glass-clad buildings have a unique characteristic: they reflect the surrounding scenery. This feature is the focus of ‘Mutedscapes’. The landscapes reflected in the mirror-like glass capture the changing times, reflected in the colors of the sky and the shapes of the clouds, allowing you to experience a new, expressive side of the building.
